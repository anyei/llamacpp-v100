(c) **eval-callback conflict** (`llama-context.cpp:1432`) — the sched has ONE eval-cb slot; any `cb_eval` (e.g. the `LLAMA_DEBUG` dump cb) displaces the SSD fill cb → streamed CPU-path experts never fill → silent garbage.
